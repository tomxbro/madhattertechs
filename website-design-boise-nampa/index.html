Why we do it

Just a few of the things that make us tick

Transparency

Simply put, we got tired of seeing our IT customers getting taken advantage of by their web site vendors. And so we decided to start offering web services that reflect the same client oriented IT services that we have been providing the Treasure Valley for years. We don’t rely on cryptic jargon to obfuscate what we do. We are dedicated to offering transparent and hassle free web services at a fair price. You will never be locked out of your own website. Your files will always be owned by you. And you can grant access to any third party IT service that you need.

Flexibility

Websites aren’t a “one size fits all” deal. So why do most companies price them as such? We provide a la carte features and functionality. So you only pay for what you want. And the great thing is that you can always decide to add a feature later on without having to redesign your entire site.
Furthermore, we don’t rely on contracts. We want customers to work with us because they enjoy and benefit from our reliable, low cost, hassle free service. Not because they’re locked into a contract.

Community

You know that saying “be the change that you want to see in the world”? Well, that pretty much explains us. It doesn’t mean we want to get the entire world singing kumbaya around the same campfire. It just means that we want to demonstrate positive business practices that benefit us, and the customer while also leaving our small corner of the world a little better than how we found it. Accordingly we support a handful of Treasure Valley non-profits that are doing some great things in the community. Whenever things start slowing down, we start looking for ways to give back.
