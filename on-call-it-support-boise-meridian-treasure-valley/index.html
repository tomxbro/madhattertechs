What We Do

Mad Hatter Techs LLC is a managed technical service company designed for small and medium size businesses. We specialize in a variety of IT related issues that help keep your business up and running safe and secure. All companies have IT needs, yet not all companies want to pay the cost of a full-time IT staff. Mad Hatter Techs fills that position in times of need with both al-a-cart support services as well as custom built packages available to accommodate your individual time and budget.
